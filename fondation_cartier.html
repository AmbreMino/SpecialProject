About

South America had a lot to offer to the art world, et this exhibition is living proof. More about architecture, inhotim and artists.



Public

Anyone can go to the exhibition.



Place

Fondation Cartier is a private institute founded in 1984 by Maison Cartier dedicated to contempory art. Initally in Jouy-en-Josas (Yvelines), it moved in 1994 to the 14th district in Paris, in a building by French architect Jean Nouvel. You really can't miss it, it's a big glass block, on several levels. For the exhibition, a geometric mural's been painted in front of the Foundation; Genesis Lunar.



Tips

The movie on the ground floor about Freddy Mamani and his work is worth your while. In the end, go up to the shop and get an overall view of the arts right under you.
